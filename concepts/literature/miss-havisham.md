# Miss Havisham

## summary

**Miss Havisham** is a wealthy, deeply wounded woman in *Great Expectations* who was abandoned at the altar and has since lived in dark seclusion, wearing her yellowed wedding dress and keeping her mansion frozen in time. She adopts the orphan Estella to raise as a tool for revenge against men, embodying the destructive power of bitterness and the failure to forgive.

## you gotta know

- Jilted on her wedding day decades before the novel begins; has not seen daylight since and wears her stained bridal gown and one shoe.
- Lives in Satis House, where the wedding feast remains untouched—rotting food, withered flowers, the clock stopped at the hour of her abandonment.
- Adopted Estella as an orphan ward and deliberately raised her to be cold, beautiful, and incapable of love, intending her to break men's hearts as revenge.
- Becomes the object of *Pip's* romantic obsession, though she encourages his hopes primarily to torment him and prove her poisoned education of Estella works.
- Dies in a fire that engulfs Satis House, her final act a moment of genuine contrition—she asks for Pip's forgiveness before perishing.
- Her poison is emotional rather than active: she corrupts through affection withheld, beauty weaponized, and the cruelty of raised false hopes.
