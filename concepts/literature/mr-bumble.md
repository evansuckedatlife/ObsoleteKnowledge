## summary

**Mr. Bumble** is the pompous, self-important workhouse master in *Oliver Twist* whose bloated dignity and misapplied authority over the poor reveal the cruelty embedded in institutional bureaucracy. A man of affected speech and inflated ego, he personifies the petty tyrant satisfied with minor power over the helpless.

## you gotta know

- Serves as the master of the workhouse where Oliver Twist is born and raised, presiding with corpulent self-satisfaction over starving children.
- Demonstrates the casual cruelty of the system through his own indifference and pomposity; he speaks in elaborate phrases to obscure rather than illuminate.
- Participates in the decision to offer a reward for Oliver's capture after the boy flees, treating his young charge as an escaped criminal.
- Later marries the matron and becomes sexton, acquiring additional petty authority which he wields with the same unthinking arrogance.
- His famous assertion, "the law is a ass, a idiot" (though technically spoken by Mr. Brownlow), captures the public perception of Bumble's ridiculous relationship to the justice system.
- Represents Dickens' attack on the New Poor Law and the officials who administered it without compassion or wisdom.
