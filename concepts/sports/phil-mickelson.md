# Phil Mickelson

## summary

*Phil Mickelson*, nicknamed "Lefty," is an American professional golfer who won 6 major championships as one of Tiger Woods's primary rivals from the 2000s through the 2020s. Known for his creative shot-making, aggressive style, and longevity competing across three decades, he became one of golf's most beloved and entertaining figures.

## you gotta know

- Won 6 major championships: 3 Masters (2004, 2006, 2010), 2 PGA Championships (2005, 2021), and 1 Open Championship (2013).
- Won the PGA Championship at age 50 in 2021, becoming the oldest winner of a major championship in professional golf history.
- Famous for his creativity and willingness to attempt flop shots and unconventional techniques that distinguished him from peers.
- Competed primarily on the PGA Tour from 1992 through 2022, demonstrating extraordinary professional longevity.
- Won 45 PGA Tour events and numerous international tournaments, establishing himself as one of the most prolific winners in modern golf.
- Engaged in one of golf's most famous rivalries with Tiger Woods throughout the 2000s–2010s.
- Known for his accessibility to fans, humor, and competitive fire that made him one of the most popular golfers of his era.
