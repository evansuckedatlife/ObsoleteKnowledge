# Novak Djokovic

## summary

*Novak Djokovic* is a Serbian tennis champion who won 24 Grand Slam singles titles, surpassing Rafael Nadal as the player with the most major championships. His exceptional defensive abilities, mental toughness, and consistency from 2011 onward made him the defining player of the 2010s and early 2020s.

## you gotta know

- Won 24 Grand Slam singles titles: 10 Australian Opens, seven Wimbledons, four US Opens, and three French Opens.
- Achieved the Calendar Year Golden Slam in 2016 (all four majors plus Olympic gold in a single year), one of only two men to achieve this.
- Known for his extraordinary defense, flexibility, and ability to hit groundstrokes from impossible positions; his return of serve is among the best ever.
- Reached world No. 1 in 2011 and held the ranking for over 370 weeks; his dominance (particularly 2011–2015 and 2018–2023) rivaled Federer's and Nadal's.
- Member of the "Big Three" with Federer and Nadal; his rivalry with both shaped tennis's last two decades.
- Made controversial pandemic-related decisions (vaccine hesitancy, missing tournaments) that affected his legacy and record, though his on-court achievements remain unmatched.
