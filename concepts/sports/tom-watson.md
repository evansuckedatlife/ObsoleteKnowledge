# Tom Watson

## summary

*Tom Watson* is an American professional golfer who won 8 major championships and emerged as one of golf's dominant players from the 1970s through the early 1980s. Known for his competitive intensity and dominance in links-style golf, he represented a generational bridge between Jack Nicklaus and later players.

## you gotta know

- Won 8 major championships: 5 Open Championships (1980, 1982, 1983, 1985, 1992), 2 Masters (1977, 1981), and 1 US Open (1982).
- Had remarkable success in The Open Championship, winning it five times and making it his signature tournament.
- Engaged in one of golf's most famous rivalries with Jack Nicklaus throughout the 1970s and early 1980s.
- Played in 32 consecutive PGA Tours, demonstrating remarkable longevity and competitive consistency.
- Known for his attacking style, mental toughness, and ability to perform under pressure in major championships.
- Won 39 PGA Tour events during his career, establishing himself as one of the tour's most consistent winners.
- Remained competitive on the Senior PGA Tour well into his later years, winning senior major championships.
