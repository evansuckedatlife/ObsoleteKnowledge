# Russell Wilson

## summary

*Russell Wilson* is an American football quarterback who spent ten seasons with the Seattle Seahawks before moving to the Denver Broncos, earning a Super Bowl championship and becoming one of the most successful dual-threat quarterbacks of the 2010s. Known for his mobility, competitiveness, and ability to extend plays outside the pocket, he led the Seahawks to multiple playoff appearances and consistent division dominance.

## you gotta know

- Drafted by the *Seattle Seahawks* in the third round (75th overall) of the 2012 NFL Draft despite being a talented prospect, becoming one of the greatest draft steals in modern NFL history.
- Won the *Super Bowl XLVIII* championship (2013 season) with the *Seahawks*, leading the team to victory after a dominant defensive performance and winning the team's first championship in franchise history.
- Named to nine *Pro Bowl* teams (2013–2016, 2018–2021) and selected to four *All-Pro* rosters, establishing himself as one of the decade's most consistent performers.
- Threw for 54,828 career passing yards and 418 touchdowns while rushing for 4,600+ yards and 51 rushing touchdowns, demonstrating elite dual-threat capability.
- Known for his scrambling ability and capacity to extend plays outside the pocket, creating opportunities through improvisation and open-field athleticism.
- Led the Seahawks to multiple NFC West division titles and playoff appearances throughout the 2010s, establishing the franchise as a perennial contender.
- Traded to the *Denver Broncos* in 2022 and subsequently moved to the *Pittsburgh Steelers*, demonstrating sustained success despite franchise changes.
