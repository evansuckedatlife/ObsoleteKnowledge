# Patrick Mahomes

## summary

*Patrick Mahomes* is an American football quarterback who emerged as the dominant force of the 2020s, leading the Kansas City Chiefs to multiple Super Bowl championships through exceptional athleticism, creativity, and clutch performances. Drafted 10th overall in 2017, he inherited the starting role in 2018 and quickly established himself as the league's most dynamic and talented player at the position.

## you gotta know

- Drafted by the *Kansas City Chiefs* in the first round (10th overall) of the 2017 NFL Draft from Texas Tech University and became the starter in 2018 following Alex Smith's injury.
- Won three *Super Bowl* championships with the Chiefs: *Super Bowl LIV* (2019 season), *Super Bowl LVII* (2022 season), and *Super Bowl LVIII* (2023 season), the most recent back-to-back championships.
- Named to six consecutive *Pro Bowl* teams (2018–2023) and won two *NFL MVP* awards (2018, 2022), along with multiple *Super Bowl MVP* awards.
- Threw for 20,000+ career passing yards and 150+ touchdowns in his first six seasons, establishing the fastest accumulation rate of any quarterback in NFL history.
- Renowned for his exceptional athleticism, arm strength, and ability to make off-platform throws while moving in any direction, revolutionizing quarterback improvisation standards.
- Known for his high-pressure, clutch performances, leading multiple playoff comebacks and championship-clinching drives throughout his career.
- Continues to lead the Chiefs in sustained excellence into the 2024 season, establishing himself as the face of modern NFL quarterback excellence and the heir to Tom Brady's legacy.
