# Mariano Rivera

## summary

**Mariano Rivera** was a closer and one of baseball's most dominant and iconic pitchers, spending nearly his entire 19-season career with the Yankees. His cut fastball was virtually unhittable, his postseason performance was unparalleled, and his quiet, dignified demeanor made him perhaps the most respected player of his generation.

## you gotta know

- Won 652 games and recorded 603 saves (fourth all-time) with a 2.75 ERA, the lowest of any reliever with 1,000+ innings pitched.
- Appeared in 13 All-Star Games and 8 World Series for the Yankees, winning 5 championships.
- Holds the record for most postseason saves (42) and most strikeouts in World Series history (11 innings).
- Threw a devastating cut fastball that opponents rarely hit successfully; his signature pitch is widely considered one of the greatest pitches ever.
- Named American League MVP for 1996 and was a near-unanimous Hall of Fame first-ballot selection in 2019.
- Nicknamed *The Sandman* for his ability to shut down games and *Mo*, beloved for his humility and leadership.
