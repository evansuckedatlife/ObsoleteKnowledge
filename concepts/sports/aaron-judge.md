# Aaron Judge

## summary

**Aaron Judge** is an outfielder and one of the Yankees' current superstars, known for his towering home runs and imposing physical presence (6'7"). His 2022 season, in which he hit 62 home runs to break the American League single-season record, revitalised Yankees fandom and cemented his place in the franchise's storied lineage.

## you gotta know

- Hit 62 home runs in 2022, breaking Roger Maris' American League single-season record set in 1961 and captivating the nation.
- Has averaged over 40 home runs per season as a Yankee, establishing himself as one of the most prolific sluggers of his generation.
- Won the American League MVP award in 2022 and was voted captain of the Yankees in 2023, a rare honour for a player in his prime.
- Is the tallest player in Major League Baseball at 6'7", giving his home runs a distinctive arc and enabling a wider strike zone.
- Has become the face of the Yankees and has drawn comparisons to Babe Ruth and Mickey Mantle for his power and home-run appeal.
- Remains a key figure in the Yankees' quest to win their first World Series since 2009.
