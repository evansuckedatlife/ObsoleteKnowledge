# Michael Vick

## summary

*Michael Vick* is an American football quarterback revolutionary for his running ability and athleticism at the position, transforming expectations of what a quarterback could accomplish with his legs. Drafted first overall by the Atlanta Falcons in 2001, he earned multiple Pro Bowl selections and defined a new archetype of dual-threat quarterback that influenced subsequent generations.

## you gotta know

- Drafted first overall by the *Atlanta Falcons* in 2001 and immediately made an impact as a dynamic playmaker combining arm strength with exceptional athleticism.
- Rushed for 1,000 yards in back-to-back seasons (2004–2005), making him one of the few quarterbacks to achieve this milestone and demonstrating his unique dual-threat threat capability.
- Named to four *Pro Bowl* teams (2004–2007) and selected to multiple All-Pro rosters during his peak years with Atlanta.
- Traded to the *Philadelphia Eagles* in 2009 following his release from Atlanta; had immediate impact while serving as a backup before injuries limited his availability.
- Became a dominant force in the 2010 season with Philadelphia, earning the *NFL Comeback Player of the Year* award and leading the Eagles to the playoffs.
- Known for his unparalleled scrambling ability and open-field improvisation, forcing defenses to defend the perimeter in ways previous quarterback designs had not required.
- Later served as a backup and journeyman, demonstrating his continued value as a game-changer even in reduced offensive roles.
