# Rory McIlroy

## summary

*Rory McIlroy* is a Northern Irish professional golfer who won 4 major championships and emerged as one of the sport's dominant players in the 2010s and 2020s. His powerful ball-striking and competitive drive established him as the leading figure in the post-Tiger Woods generation of golfers.

## you gotta know

- Won 4 major championships: 1 US Open (2011), 3 PGA Championships (2016, 2019, 2023).
- Won the US Open at age 22 in 2011, the second-youngest winner in the tournament's history.
- Demonstrated sustained excellence across the 2010s–2020s, competing at the highest level for over a decade.
- Known for his exceptional driving distance and accuracy off the tee, which established his competitive advantage.
- Won 23 PGA Tour events and numerous international tournaments, establishing himself as one of the most prolific winners in modern golf.
- Competed in multiple Ryder Cup competitions, representing Europe and demonstrating his competitive commitment to team golf.
- Emerged as one of golf's most prominent global figures after Tiger Woods's declining years, representing the next generation.
