# Jordan Spieth

## summary

*Jordan Spieth* is an American professional golfer who won 3 major championships and emerged as one of the sport's brightest young talents in the 2010s. Known for his short-game brilliance and aggressive style, he represented a new generation of golfers competing at the highest levels of the sport.

## you gotta know

- Won 3 major championships: 2 Masters (2015, 2022), 1 US Open (2015), and 1 Open Championship (2017).
- Won the Masters and US Open in the same calendar year (2015) as a 21-year-old, establishing himself as an elite talent.
- Known for his exceptional chipping and short-game creativity, skills that distinguished him from peers.
- Captured the attention of American golf fans as a homegrown star with competitive intensity and dedication.
- Won 13 PGA Tour events and numerous international tournaments during his career.
- Competed professionally from 2013 onward, establishing himself as one of the most significant American golfers of the 2010s generation.
- Demonstrated remarkable consistency in major championships despite periods of competitive struggle and self-doubt.
