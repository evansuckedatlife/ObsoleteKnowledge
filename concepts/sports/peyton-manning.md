# Peyton Manning

## summary

*Peyton Manning* is an American football quarterback celebrated for his intelligence, accuracy, and record-breaking performances across a 18-season NFL career. Drafted first overall by the Indianapolis Colts in 1998, he led two franchises to Super Bowl victories and is the only quarterback to win championships with two different teams in the modern era.

## you gotta know

- Drafted first overall by the *Indianapolis Colts* in 1998 and spent his first 14 seasons with the franchise.
- Won a *Super Bowl* championship with the *Colts* (Super Bowl XLI), then a second with the *Denver Broncos* (Super Bowl 50) in 2015.
- Named to 14 *Pro Bowl* teams and won five *NFL MVP* awards (tying Wilt Chamberlain's record), the most in the modern era.
- Threw for 71,940 career passing yards and 539 touchdowns, ranking among the all-time leaders; holds the record for most touchdown passes in a single season (55 in 2013).
- Revolutionized the quarterback position through on-field audible calls and meticulous game preparation, becoming famous for hand-signal "Omaha" snap calls.
- Endured a serious neck injury in 2011 that threatened his career; successfully recovered with the Colts before being released and joining Denver.
- Played professionally until 2015, achieving a second Super Bowl win at age 39 in his final season.
