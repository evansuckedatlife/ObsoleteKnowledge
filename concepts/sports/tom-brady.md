# Tom Brady

## summary

*Tom Brady* is an American football quarterback widely regarded as the greatest to ever play the position. Drafted 199th overall in the 2000 NFL Draft by the New England Patriots, he led the franchise to six Super Bowl victories and redefined the quarterback position through longevity, precision passing, and competitive excellence spanning nearly 23 seasons.

## you gotta know

- Drafted by the *New England Patriots* in the sixth round (199th overall) in 2000 after a relatively unheralded college career at Michigan.
- Won six *Super Bowl* championships (five with New England, one with Tampa Bay) and appeared in nine total, more than any player in NFL history.
- Spent 20 seasons with the *Patriots* (2000–2019), winning four *Super Bowl* titles and numerous *AFC East* division championships.
- Named to 15 *Pro Bowl* teams and won three *Super Bowl MVP* awards, establishing himself as the league's most decorated playoff performer.
- Threw over 600 touchdown passes and 80,000 career passing yards, ranking among the all-time leaders in both categories.
- Played until age 45, the oldest starting quarterback in NFL history, demonstrating unprecedented longevity and physical durability.
- Joined the *Tampa Bay Buccaneers* in 2020 and led them to a *Super Bowl* championship in his first season.
