# Aaron Rodgers

## summary

*Aaron Rodgers* is an American football quarterback widely praised for his exceptional arm talent and clutch performances throughout a 20-season career with the Green Bay Packers. Drafted 24th overall in 2005, he emerged as Brett Favre's successor and led the Packers to a Super Bowl championship while establishing himself as one of the most talented players at the position.

## you gotta know

- Drafted by the *Green Bay Packers* in the first round (24th overall) of the 2005 NFL Draft and served as Brett Favre's backup before becoming the starter in 2006.
- Won the *Super Bowl XLV* championship (2010 season) with the *Packers*, leading the franchise to victory and winning the *Super Bowl MVP* award.
- Named to 10 *Pro Bowl* teams (2011–2015, 2017–2020) and won one *NFL MVP* award (2011), establishing himself as an elite performer in the position.
- Threw for 59,056 career passing yards and 475 touchdowns in his time with Green Bay, ranking among the franchise leaders in both categories.
- Renowned for his exceptional arm strength and accuracy, capable of making throws from unconventional arm angles and delivering balls with precision from anywhere on the field.
- Known for his "Hail Mary" throws and clutch fourth-quarter performances, including several improbable playoff wins and game-winning drives throughout his career.
- Remained with the Packers through 2021 before finishing his career with the New York Jets, defining Green Bay quarterback excellence in the post-Favre era.
