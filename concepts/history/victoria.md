# Victoria

## summary

Victoria was Queen of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland from 1837 to 1901, and Empress of India from 1876. Her 63-year reign, known as the Victorian Era, was a period of immense industrial, political, scientific, and military change, as well as the height of the British Empire's global power.

## you gotta know

- She adopted the title *Empress of India* in 1876, championed by her favorite prime minister, Benjamin Disraeli.
- She wore black mourning attire for the final forty years of her life following the premature death of her husband, *Prince Albert*, in 1861.
- She was known as the *Grandmother of Europe* because her nine children married into royal families across the continent, spreading the genetic trait of hemophilia.
- She survived at least seven assassination attempts during her reign, including several shootings.
- Her reign of 63 years and 7 months was the longest in British history until she was surpassed by her great-great-granddaughter Elizabeth II.
