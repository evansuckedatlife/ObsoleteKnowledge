# National Geographic

## summary

*National Geographic Magazine*, founded in 1888 by the *National Geographic Society*, is an illustrated journal of exploration, natural science, and world culture. Famous for its striking photography, exotic travel narratives, and educational mission, it has shaped how Americans understand the natural world and distant cultures.

## you gotta know

- Founded in 1888 by the National Geographic Society; began as a scholarly journal and evolved into a mass-market magazine.
- Circulation grew to nearly *12 million* in the late twentieth century; translated into multiple languages and distributed worldwide.
- Famous for *color photography* of nature and remote places; elevated magazine photography to an art form and scientific documentation tool.
- Published photographs by renowned photographers including *Robert Capa*, *Steve Winter*, and *Joel Sartore*.
- Known for expedition documentation — funding and chronicling explorations of Mount Everest, the Galápagos, and polar regions.
- Editorial mission combined scientific education, cultural documentation, and adventurous storytelling about the natural world and human societies.
- The distinctive yellow-bordered cover became iconic and instantly recognizable.
