# Getúlio Vargas

## summary

**Getúlio Dornelles Vargas** (1882–1954) was a Brazilian military officer and politician who dominated Brazilian politics for 24 years, serving as president during both democratic and authoritarian periods. He modernized Brazil's economy and labour system while establishing a paternalistic, populist style of governance.

## you gotta know

- Led a military coup in 1930 and took power, beginning the *Vargas Era* (1930–1954).
- Established the Estado Novo (New State) in 1937, a fascist-influenced authoritarian regime that lasted until 1945.
- Implemented labour reforms including the minimum wage, workers' compensation, and a labour code modeled on Italian fascism.
- Initially supported Nazi Germany and Vichy France but switched allegiance to the Allies in World War II.
- Returned to power democratically in 1950 and faced mounting political pressures; he committed suicide in 1954 while still president.
- Remained popular among the working class despite his authoritarianism, and left a controversial legacy.
