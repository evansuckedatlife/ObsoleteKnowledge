# Burt Rutan

## summary

*Burt Rutan* is an American aircraft designer and engineer who pioneered composite aircraft construction and designed innovative experimental aircraft including the *Voyager*, which flew around the world nonstop without refueling. His engineering vision expanded the boundaries of what was aeronautically possible.

## you gotta know

- Designed the *Voyager*, which circled the world in 1986 in nine days without refueling—a record-breaking feat of endurance.
- Pioneered composite aircraft construction, using advanced materials to reduce weight and increase efficiency.
- Founded Scaled Composites in 1982, a company that designed experimental aircraft and pushed aeronautical frontiers.
- Designed *SpaceShipOne*, which became the first privately-developed spacecraft to reach the edge of space in 2004.
- Developed the *VariViggen*, an unusual canard-configuration aircraft that demonstrated unconventional design principles.
- Won the Ansari X Prize in 2004 with SpaceShipOne, spurring commercial spaceflight development.
- Continued designing aircraft into the 21st century, blending aeronautical and aerospace innovation.
