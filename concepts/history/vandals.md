# Vandals

## summary

The *Vandals* were a Germanic people who established a powerful naval kingdom in North Africa under *Gaiseric*, notorious for raiding and sacking Rome itself in 455. Their mastery of Mediterranean piracy and conquest of Roman grain-producing provinces made them among the most feared of the Germanic successor states, until Byzantine reconquest destroyed them in the 6th century.

## you gotta know

- Sacked Rome in 455 under *Gaiseric*, systematically plundering the city and targeting the wealth of the Roman elite—an even more thorough devastation than Alaric's sack 45 years earlier.
- Conquered North Africa, including the rich province of Africa Proconsularis, cutting off grain supplies to Rome and destabilizing the Western Empire's economy.
- Controlled a powerful fleet that dominated the Mediterranean, raiding coasts across the Roman world and extracting tribute from major powers.
- *Gaiseric* established a centralized kingdom based in Carthage, consolidating Vandal rule and passing the throne to his heirs in a stable succession.
- Practiced extreme religious intolerance toward Catholic Romans, favoring Arian Christianity and persecuting Catholic populations—a legacy that darkened their reputation in later Christian chroniclers.
- Fell to *Justinian I's* reconquest (533–534) under the general *Belisarius*; the Vandal kingdom collapsed rapidly and was absorbed into the Byzantine Empire.
- The term "vandalism" (senseless destruction) derives from later stereotypes of their destructiveness, though modern historians see the kingdom as more sophisticated than the term suggests.

## connections

- [[gaiseric]] — the king who transformed the Vandals from raiders into a major Mediterranean power.
- [[roman-empire]] — their chief rival and victim; they hastened its final decline in the west.
- [[north-africa]] — their primary settlement and kingdom base, conquered from Roman control.
- [[mediterranean]] — the sea they dominated and used as a highway for conquest and plunder.
- [[justinian]] — the Byzantine emperor whose reconquest ended the Vandal kingdom.
