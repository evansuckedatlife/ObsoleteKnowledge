# The Barber of Seville

## summary

Gioachino Rossini's *The Barber of Seville* (1816) is a two-act *opera buffa* (comic opera) that stands as a masterpiece of the Italian *bel canto* style. Based on Pierre Beaumarchais's play *Le Barbier de Séville*, the opera serves as a prequel to the events of *The Marriage of Figaro*. The plot centers on Count Almaviva, who has fallen in love with the wealthy young Rosina. To win her heart without her knowing his high status, he disguises himself as the poor student "Lindoro." With the help of Figaro, the clever town barber and jack-of-all-trades, Almaviva infiltrates the house of Rosina's suspicious, elderly guardian, Doctor Bartolo, who plans to marry her himself. Through a series of disguises, forged documents, and bribed music teachers, the lovers succeed in marrying just before Bartolo can stop them.

## you gotta know

- **Figaro** is the title character, a charismatic, quick-witted barber and matchmaker who schemes to get Almaviva into Bartolo's house.
- **Count Almaviva** is the wealthy suitor who adopts the alias **Lindoro** to ensure Rosina loves him for himself, later disguising himself as a drunken soldier and a music teacher named Don Alonso.
- **Rosina** is Doctor Bartolo's clever and defiant ward who is kept locked away but successfully outwits her guardian to communicate with Lindoro.
- **Doctor Bartolo** is the paranoid doctor and Rosina's guardian who aims to secure her large inheritance by forcing her to marry him.
- **Largo al factotum** (*Make way for the servant*) is Figaro's rapid-fire Act I entrance aria, famous for its tongue-twisting delivery, high energy, and repetitions of "Figaro!"
- **Una voce poco fa** (*A voice just now*) is Rosina's Act I cavatina, a showpiece of vocal agility in which she declares her love for Lindoro but warns that she can play a hundred tricks if crossed.
- **La calunnia è un venticello** (*Slander is a gentle breeze*) is sung by the music teacher **Don Basilio**, describing how a rumor starts as a whisper and builds into a cannon blast to ruin Almaviva's reputation.
