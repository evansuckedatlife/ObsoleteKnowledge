## summary

King of *Pylos* and the eldest statesman of the Greek forces at Troy, Nestor earned respect through his wisdom and experience rather than martial prowess. He served as counselor and peacemaker, mediating disputes and offering strategic advice that shaped the Greeks' approach to the ten-year war.

## you gotta know

- Oldest warrior among the Greeks, a survivor of previous generations' conflicts
- King of Pylos and one of the most respected voices in Greek councils
- Repeatedly mediated between *Agamemnon* and *Achilles* during their quarrels
- Known for his lengthy speeches filled with historical references and wisdom
- Fought bravely despite his age, standing firm against *Hector* in multiple encounters
- Escaped Troy and returned home successfully after the war
- Featured prominently in *Homer's Odyssey* as a living link to the heroic past
