# Jason

## summary

**Jason** was the rightful heir to the throne of *Thessaly*, robbed of his kingdom in infancy. He led the most famous expedition in Greek mythology—the voyage of the *Argonauts*—sailing aboard the ship *Argo* in search of the *Golden Fleece*. His leadership united the greatest heroes of his age, though his story ends in tragedy and obscurity.

## you gotta know

- Raised in secret by the centaur *Chiron* after being hidden from his uncle, the usurper *Pelias*.
- Assembled a crew of legendary heroes, including *Heracles*, *Theseus*, and *Atalanta*, to sail the *Argo*.
- Journeyed to the far edge of the world seeking the *Golden Fleece*, guarded by a dragon in the land of *Colchis*.
- Won the fleece with the aid of *Medea*, a sorceress princess who fell in love with him and used magic to help him succeed.
- Returned home and reclaimed his throne, but his abandonment of *Medea* later brought her terrible vengeance.
- His legend represents both the glory of heroic adventure and the cost of ambition without loyalty.

## connections

- [[heracles]] — the most famous of the Argonauts; sailed under Jason's command.
- [[theseus]] — fellow Argonaut; both were heroes of the first rank.
- [[atalanta]] — the only woman among the Argonauts; brave huntress and warrior.
- [[medea]] — the princess who saved him; their troubled relationship defines both their fates.
- [[medusa]] — the Gorgon; Medea's path crossed with her through mythology's threads.
- [[chiron]] — the wise centaur who raised him in secret.
- [[pelias]] — the uncle who usurped his throne and set him on his quest.
