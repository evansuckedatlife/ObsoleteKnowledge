# Heimdall

## summary

*Heimdall* is the watchman of the gods, eternally vigilant at the entrance to *Asgard*. Born of nine mothers and sensitive to every disturbance across the nine worlds, he guards the rainbow bridge *Bifrost* and will sound the war-horn *Gjallarhorn* to announce the beginning of *Ragnarok*.

## you gotta know

- The eternal guardian of *Bifrost*, the rainbow bridge connecting Asgard to the other worlds; nothing escapes his notice.
- Possesses superhuman senses: he can hear grass growing and see as far as day and night; he requires less sleep than a bird.
- Father of humanity through his mortal guises; legends say he taught men and women the social structures of society.
- Will blow the *Gjallarhorn* with a blast so loud it resonates through all nine worlds, signaling the start of Ragnarok.
- Will fight *Loki* in single combat at the end of the world; they will slay each other, representing order's final stand against chaos.
- Born of nine mothers and therefore connected to the *norns*, fate itself, and the deep magic of creation.

## connections

- [[balder]] — the slain god whose death he witnessed and grieves.
- [[norns]] — the weavers of fate with whom he shares deep mystical ties.
