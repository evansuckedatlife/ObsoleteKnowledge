# Theseus

## summary

**Theseus** was the legendary king of *Athens* and one of the greatest heroes of ancient Greece. Known for his cunning as much as his courage, he defeated the fearsome *Minotaur* in the labyrinth of *Crete* and united the scattered tribes of *Attica* under a single rule. His story embodies the civilizing force of a just leader.

## you gotta know

- Son of *Aegeus*, king of *Athens*, though some claim *Poseidon* was his true father.
- Slew the *Minotaur*, a hybrid creature half-man, half-bull, in the depths of a vast labyrinth designed by *Daedalus*.
- Escaped the labyrinth with the help of *Ariadne*, who gave him a ball of thread to mark his path back.
- United the twelve independent communities of *Attica* into the Athenian state, making him Athens's founder-king.
- Sailed with *Jason* and the *Argonauts* on their quest for the *Golden Fleece*.
- Famously abandoned *Ariadne* on the island of *Naxos* after escaping Crete, though the reasons vary between sources.
