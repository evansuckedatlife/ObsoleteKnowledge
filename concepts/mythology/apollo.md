# Apollo

## summary

Apollo is one of the most complex and important Olympian deities in classical Greek and Roman mythology. He is the god of archery, music, dance, truth, prophecy, healing, diseases, poetry, and the sun. The twin brother of Artemis, Apollo was born on the island of Delos and is famously associated with his primary sanctuary at Delphi, home of the Pythian oracle.

## you gotta know

- He established the *Oracle of Delphi* after slaying the giant serpent *Python*, which was sent by Hera to persecute his pregnant mother, Leto.
- He cursed the Trojan princess *Cassandra* with the burden of accurate prophecies that would never be believed, after she rejected his romantic advances.
- He was born along with his twin sister *Artemis* on the floating island of *Delos*, the only land that would grant asylum to their mother Leto.
- He flayed the satyr *Marsyas* alive after Marsyas challenged him to a musical contest and lost.
- He accidentally killed his male lover *Hyacinthus* with a stray discus, transforming his spilled blood into the hyacinth flower.
- He was pursuing the nymph *Daphne* when she prayed for escape and was transformed into a *laurel tree*, which subsequently became the god's sacred plant.
