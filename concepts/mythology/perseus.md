## summary

**Perseus** was the son of *Zeus* and the mortal princess *Danaë*, raised in exile and destined for greatness. He is most renowned for slaying *Medusa*, the terrifying Gorgon whose gaze turned men to stone, using only his wits and divine gifts. His triumph over impossible odds ranks him among the greatest heroes of Greek mythology.

## you gotta know

- Born after *Zeus* seduced his mother, *Danaë*, who was locked away in a bronze tower by her fearful father.
- Received divine aid from *Athena* and *Hermes*, who gifted him a magical sickle and winged sandals to hunt *Medusa*.
- Beheaded *Medusa* while she slept, using his polished shield as a mirror to avoid her deadly gaze.
- Used Medusa's severed head as a weapon, which retained its petrifying power even in death.
- Rescued the princess *Andromeda* from a sea monster sent by *Poseidon* and married her.
- Accidentally caused the death of his grandfather, fulfilling an ancient prophecy despite efforts to prevent it.
