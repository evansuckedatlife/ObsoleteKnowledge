# Lancelot

## summary

Sir Lancelot du Lac stands as the greatest knight of the *Round Table*—unmatched in courage and martial prowess—yet his love for Queen *Guinevere* becomes the tragic flaw that brings down the kingdom he served so faithfully. His story exemplifies the medieval conflict between chivalric duty and human desire.

## you gotta know

- Raised by the *Lady of the Lake* in the magical realm of *Lac*, hence his epithet "du Lac"
- Widely considered the strongest and most skilled warrior of Arthur's knights, renowned for quests and tournaments
- Fathered *Galahad* with Elaine, a union orchestrated through deception, producing the knight destined to achieve the *Holy Grail*
- Carried on a passionate, ultimately public affair with Queen *Guinevere*, violating his oath to Arthur
- His rescue of Guinevere from execution by burning sparked civil war among the Round Table knights
- Driven into exile after Camelot's collapse, some traditions claim he became a hermit or monk in his final years
- Represents the paradox of the perfect knight destroyed not by weakness but by the nobility of his own heart
