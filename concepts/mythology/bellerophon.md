# Bellerophon

## summary

**Bellerophon** was a warrior of noble birth who became the hero most renowned for slaying the *Chimera*, a fire-breathing monster with the head of a lion, body of a goat, and tail of a serpent. Riding the divine winged horse *Pegasus*, he achieved victories that seemed impossible, only to be brought low by hubris and divine punishment.

## you gotta know

- Grandson of *Sisyphus*; his early life marked by exile and false accusations of attempted seduction.
- Tamed the divine winged horse *Pegasus*, born from the blood of *Medusa* after her death.
- Defeated the *Chimera*, a fire-breathing hybrid monster, by riding *Pegasus* and striking from above with a spear.
- Completed seemingly impossible tasks set by a jealous king, including fighting the *Amazons* and battling fierce warriors.
- Grew proud of his accomplishments and attempted to fly to *Mount Olympus* on *Pegasus* to join the gods.
- Was thrown from *Pegasus* by *Zeus* for his arrogance; he fell to earth and lived out his days blind and lame.

## connections
