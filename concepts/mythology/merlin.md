## summary

The legendary wizard and advisor to King Arthur, credited with orchestrating Arthur's birth and rise to power through magic and prophecy. Merlin embodies the liminal space between the mortal and magical worlds, his counsel essential to Camelot's founding yet ultimately unable to prevent its destruction.

## you gotta know

- A powerful *enchanter* and *prophet* whose magic enabled Arthur's legitimacy and the pulling of *Excalibur* from the stone
- Arranged the deception that led to Arthur's conception, engineering a union between *Uther Pendragon* and *Ygraine*
- Served as Arthur's first and wisest advisor, guiding political and military decisions
- Built or enchanted *Camelot* itself and shaped the *Round Table's* magical properties
- Often portrayed as trapped or withdrawn from the world in his later years, whether imprisoned, lost in the forest, or bound by love or magic
- His fate varies across traditions: some say he was trapped by the *Lady of the Lake*, others that he fell in love with *Morgan le Fay* or her student and was enchanted away
- Represents the tension between knowledge/magic and the inability to control human nature and destiny
