# Heracles

## summary

The strongest man who ever lived, **Heracles** was the illegitimate son of *Zeus* and the mortal woman *Alcmene*. He is famous above all for completing the *Twelve Labors*, a series of seemingly impossible tasks imposed on him as penance for a tragic madness sent by the jealous *Hera*. His legend embodies the triumph of strength and perseverance over impossible odds.

## you gotta know

- Driven mad by *Hera* (jealous of his birth), he killed his own family; the *Twelve Labors* were his redemption.
- Slew the *Nemean Lion*, whose hide could not be pierced by any weapon, and wore it as armor ever after.
- Defeated the *Lernaean Hydra*, a many-headed serpent that grew two heads for every one cut off.
- Captured the *Golden Hind of Artemis* and brought back *Cerberus*, the three-headed guard dog of the Underworld.
- Married the divine princess *Deianeira*; her gift of a seemingly magical robe accidentally poisoned him to death.
- Achieved godhood and joined the *Olympians* after his death, becoming a full immortal.
