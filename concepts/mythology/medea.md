## summary

A powerful sorceress and princess of Colchis who became legendary for her passionate love, her mastery of magic, and her terrible vengeance. She helped *Jason* obtain the Golden Fleece but later committed heinous acts when abandoned, becoming a tragic and fearsome figure.

## you gotta know

- Princess of *Colchis* and daughter of *Aeetes*, trained in the magical arts and venoms by the gods themselves.
- Fell madly in love with *Jason* when he arrived seeking the *Golden Fleece*, abandoning her loyalty to her father for passion.
- Used her magical knowledge to help *Jason* complete the seemingly impossible tasks set by her father, including taming fire-breathing bulls.
- Fled *Colchis* with *Jason* and the Argonauts, even murdering her own brother *Apsyrtus* to delay her father's pursuit.
- Married *Jason* and bore him children, but he later abandoned her for a political marriage to the princess of Corinth.
- In her rage at betrayal, she murdered her own children and *Jason's* new bride with fire, then fled to *Athens*.
- Exemplifies the destructive power of unchecked emotion, magic, and the consequences of abandonment in Greek tragedy.

## connections

- [[jason]] — the hero she loved and helped, then devastated when he betrayed her.
- [[the-argonauts]] — the legendary quest she enabled through her magic and cunning.
- [[theseus]] — the Athenian king who gave her refuge after her vengeance in Corinth.
- [[zeus]] — invoked in her magical oaths, and her ancestry traces to divine lineage.
