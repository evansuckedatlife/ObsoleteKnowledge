# Lernaean Hydra

## summary

A serpentine monstrosity with nine heads—eight mortal and one immortal—the *Lernaean Hydra* terrorized the marshes of Lerna in Argos. Slaying this monster formed the second labor of *Heracles*, who discovered that cutting off one head caused two more to grow back, forcing him to employ both fire and cleverness to finally defeat it.

## you gotta know

- One of the nine heads was immortal and could not be killed by ordinary means, requiring special treatment to permanently defeat
- Each head regrew as two new heads whenever one was severed, making straightforward combat increasingly hopeless
- The creature's breath and blood were so venomous that the very fumes could kill a man, and its fangs dripped with lethal poison
- *Heracles* eventually realized he needed to sear each neck stump with fire to prevent regeneration while attacking the immortal head
- The immortal head was buried under a massive rock after being severed, keeping it permanently imprisoned
- The monster's parent was usually said to be *Echidna* and *Typhon*, making it kin to other legendary beasts like the *Chimera*
- Its lair in the Lernaean marshes was so mephitic that simply approaching it could sicken a traveler
- The second labor was considered far more challenging than the first (the *Nemean Lion*), pushing *Heracles* to use intellect alongside brute strength
