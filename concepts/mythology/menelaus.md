## summary

King of *Sparta* and husband of *Helen*, Menelaus was the nominal cause of the Trojan War when *Paris* abducted his wife. Though less prominent than *Agamemnon* or *Achilles* in the fighting, he remained a capable warrior and the war's ultimate victor, eventually reclaiming Helen and the Greek cause.

## you gotta know

- Married *Helen*, whose abduction by *Paris* triggered the ten-year war
- Co-led the Greek forces alongside his brother *Agamemnon* from Sparta
- Challenged Paris to single combat in the *Iliad*, though the duel was interrupted
- Survived the war and famously retrieved Helen, though their reunion was complicated
- Fought with *Nestor* and other Greek heroes but lacked the superhuman martial prowess of *Achilles*
- Protected Ajax's funeral games and played a role in distributing war prizes
- Honored in Homer's *Odyssey* as a successful returning home after the war's end
