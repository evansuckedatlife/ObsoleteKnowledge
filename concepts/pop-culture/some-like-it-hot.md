# Some Like It Hot

## summary

*Some Like It Hot* is a 1959 comedy masterpiece directed by *Billy Wilder*, following two musicians who go undercover as women in an all-female jazz band to escape gangsters. Starring *Tony Curtis*, *Jack Lemmon*, and *Marilyn Monroe*, the film balances slapstick humor with sophisticated wit and remains the quintessential Hollywood comedy.

## you gotta know

- Directed by *Billy Wilder*, starring *Tony Curtis* and *Jack Lemmon* as Joe and Jerry, with *Marilyn Monroe* as Sugar Kane.
- Set in the Prohibition era; Joe and Jerry witness a mob hit and flee Chicago disguised as women in an orchestra.
- The film's final line—delivered by Lemmon's character in response to being revealed as male—is considered one of cinema's perfect comedic payoffs.
- Won the Academy Award for Best Costume Design; nominated for Best Picture, Best Director, and Best Actress (Monroe).
- Features memorable musical performances, including Monroe's rendition of "I Wanna Be Loved by You," a seminal moment in her career.
- The chemistry between Lemmon and Curtis, and their physical comedy, carries the film's absurdist premise with charm and inventiveness.

## connections
