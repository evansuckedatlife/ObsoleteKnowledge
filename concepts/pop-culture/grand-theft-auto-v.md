# Grand Theft Auto V

## summary

**Grand Theft Auto V** is a crime-sandbox developed by Rockstar Games, released in 2013 and set in the sprawling fictional metropolis of *Los Santos*. Players control three protagonists—*Michael, Franklin, and Trevor*—completing missions, heists, and open-world activities in one of gaming's most detailed cities. It became a cultural touchstone, with both the single-player campaign and its multiplayer component, *GTA Online*, dominating sales and streaming for over a decade.

## you gotta know

- Interweaves three playable characters whose personal arcs and motivations intersect across a branching narrative of crime and corruption.
- Massive open city with diverse districts, each with distinct architecture, NPCs, and activities—from street racing to stock manipulation to scuba diving.
- Introduces the *Heist* mission format: multi-stage criminal operations with varying approaches and consequences; planning phase affects what equipment and allies are available.
- *GTA Online* (launched 2013) evolved into a persistent multiplayer sandbox with player properties, businesses, and continuous content updates; generated billions in revenue.
- Gameplay loop centers on driving, shooting, stealth, and exploration; side activities include tennis, golf, yoga, and property investment that generates in-game income.
- Features voice acting and mission design that mimic cinematic storytelling; features licensed music across multiple radio stations.
- Criticized for portrayal of violence and for GTA Online's shift toward monetization, but universally praised for technical polish and sheer world detail.
