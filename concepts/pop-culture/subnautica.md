# Subnautica

## summary

**Subnautica** is an underwater survival sandbox developed by Unknown Worlds Entertainment, released in early access in 2014 and fully released in 2018. Players crash-land on an alien ocean planet and must explore vast underwater biomes, gather resources, construct bases, and uncover the planet's secrets while managing oxygen, depth limits, and aggressive fauna. Its sense of discovery and environmental storytelling define a unique open-world experience.

## you gotta know

- Alien ocean world is divided into biomes (Safe Shallows, Kelp Forest, Reefs, Abyss) accessible through increasing depth as players upgrade diving equipment and submarine technology.
- Base-building is core: players construct underwater habitats modularly, add biodomes for farming, and expand infrastructure to improve oxygen, power, and storage as depth increases.
- Resource gathering loops progression: collecting raw materials like copper, gold, and titanium enables crafted upgrades (fins, tanks, submarines); scarcity forces exploration.
- Submarines (*Seamoth*, *Prawn Suit*, *Neptune*) evolve as tools for deeper exploration and defend against or escape aggressive creatures like the iconic *Reaper Leviathan*.
- Narrative unfolds through radio messages, habitat PDAs (personal data assistants), and environmental clues; players piece together how they crash-landed and what the planet's history entails.
- Manages anxiety and wonder: the *Unknown Depths* system generates tension (deep water, dangerous creatures, oxygen depletion) balanced by the tranquility of coral gardens and musical ambiance.
- Sequel *Subnautica: Below Zero* (2021) expands mechanics with surface exploration, vehicles, and a land-based story arc on a different ice-locked planet.
