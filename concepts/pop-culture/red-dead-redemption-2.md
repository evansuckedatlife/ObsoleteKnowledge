# Red Dead Redemption 2

## summary

**Red Dead Redemption 2** is an open-world Western developed by Rockstar Games, released in 2018 for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One. Players control *Arthur Morgan*, an outlaw confronting his mortality as the era of the Old West collapses. Its meticulous recreation of 1899 America, cinematic storytelling, and emergent open-world systems set a benchmark for narrative-driven sandbox games.

## you gotta know

- Takes place before the original *Red Dead Redemption*; chronicles the decline of the *Van der Linde Gang* through 137 missions across a vast, richly detailed frontier.
- World simulation emphasizes immersion: NPCs have daily routines, horses require grooming and food, weapons need cleaning, camps develop over time as the gang's fortune changes.
- Gunplay is methodical and weighted—shooting feels deliberate, with reload times and weapon sway reflecting Arthur's condition; gun duels are tense, ritualistic encounters.
- Hunting and crafting systems reward exploration; players stalk animals, skin them for pelts and meat, and craft upgrades to camps, saddles, and supplies.
- Dynamic events litter the world: ambushes, accidents, moral choices (save a drowning stranger, side with bandits or lawmen) that alter reputation and unlock alternate perspectives.
- *Red Dead Online* (multiplayer component) launched post-launch with persistent player characters and seasonal content, though updates slowed compared to *GTA Online*.
- Balances linear narrative cinematic sequences with open-world freedom; mission design is less forgiving of player experimentation than *GTA V*, demanding specific outcomes.
