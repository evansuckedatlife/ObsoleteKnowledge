# Terraria

## summary

**Terraria** is a 2D sandbox survival game developed by Re-Logic, released in 2011 for PC. Players explore an underground world, gathering resources, crafting tools and weapons, building shelter, and battling monsters and bosses. Often called "2D Minecraft" for its mechanics, it carves its own niche through boss encounters, exploration depth, and accessibility, spawning a devoted community and numerous updates over more than a decade.

## you gotta know

- Procedurally generated 2D world of varying themes: forests, deserts, underground jungles, corruption biomes, and floating islands accessible through grappling and mobility items.
- Crafting at benches and special stations creates progression from basic wooden tools to advanced magic weapons and armor; over 4,000 items to craft or loot.
- Boss encounters serve as major milestones: *Eye of Cthulhu*, *Eater of Worlds*, *Skeletron* gate progression through the underground, with harder bosses unlocking new areas and drops.
- Multiplayer supports up to 8 players on a shared world; permadeath *Hardcore* mode challenges veteran players.
- Building and decoration are central; players construct elaborate houses for NPCs (which unlock vendors and services) and personalize bases with themed architecture.
- Pixel-art aesthetic and expansive soundtrack created by Andrew Spinks (who also leads design), evolving through multiple major free updates adding new bosses, items, and mechanics.
- Harder than *Minecraft*; boss fights require learning patterns and preparing adequate gear, creating a progression arc distinct from pure sandbox creativity.
