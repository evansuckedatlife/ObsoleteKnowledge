# The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

## summary

**The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild** is an action-adventure game developed by Nintendo, released in 2017 for Nintendo Switch and Wii U. Set in the kingdom of *Hyrule*, players control *Link* awakening from a century-long slumber to defeat *Calamity Ganon* and rescue *Princess Zelda*. Its revolutionary open-world design—freedom to approach objectives in any order—redefined sandbox exploration and proved Nintendo's mastery of environmental storytelling.

## you gotta know

- *Go anywhere, do anything* philosophy: players can attempt the final boss fight immediately upon leaving the initial tutorial area, or spend hundreds of hours exploring.
- Durability system on weapons forces players to adapt; nearly every weapon breaks after use, encouraging experimentation rather than reliance on single gear sets.
- Interactive physics engine allows creative problem-solving: climb any surface, use fire to create updrafts, combine ingredients and items in unexpected ways.
- Four Divine Beasts (dungeons) correspond to elemental powers; each can be tackled in any order, and solutions are often emergent from the environment rather than puzzle-lock based.
- Shrines (small dungeons) scattered across the overworld grant ability upgrades and encourage exploration; 120 shrines provide optional challenges and shortcuts.
- Environmental clues replace quest markers; players read the landscape—smoke from monster camps, distant towers, NPC hints—to navigate and discover secrets.
- Became a system-seller for Switch and won countless Game of the Year awards; spawned the "Zelda-like" (open-world, player-driven) design philosophy in the industry.
