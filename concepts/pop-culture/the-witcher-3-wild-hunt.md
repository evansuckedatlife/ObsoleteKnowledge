# The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

## summary

**The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt** is an action role-playing game developed by CD Projekt Red, released in 2015 for PC, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. Players assume the role of *Geralt of Rivia*, a witcher (monster hunter), pursuing the villainous *Wild Hunt* across a richly detailed fantasy world while searching for his lost love. Its branching narrative, morally complex characters, and expansive quests elevated storytelling in open-world games.

## you gotta know

- Narrative branches heavily on player choices: dialogue options alter quest outcomes, NPC fates, and ending; minor decisions ripple across the story in unexpected ways.
- Three expansions—*Hearts of Stone* and *Blood and Wine*—add 30+ hours of content and are praised as equal to the base game in quality; many consider them superior.
- Monster hunting drives exploration: players research creatures, gather specific oils and potions, and exploit weaknesses; combat requires preparation and learning patterns.
- Alchemy and crafting systems reward potion management; players must balance carrying weight and strategically prepare for tough encounters.
- Side quests are narrative-rich; even minor tasks have compelling backstories, moral dilemmas, and character development absent from routine quests in other games.
- The *Bloody Baron* quest became a talking point for its emotionally mature storytelling, subverting typical fantasy tropes and exploring domestic abuse and loss.
- Published in Poland by CD Projekt Red (not a mainstream Western studio at the time), proving independent studios could rival AAA narrative ambition.
