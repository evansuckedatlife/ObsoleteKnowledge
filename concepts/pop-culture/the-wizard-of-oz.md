# The Wizard of Oz

## summary

*The Wizard of Oz* is a 1939 fantasy musical following a Kansas farm girl named *Dorothy* who is transported by tornado to the magical land of Oz, where she embarks on a journey to find her way home. Directed by *Victor Fleming* and starring *Judy Garland*, the film combines spectacular production design, memorable songs, and emotional depth to create an enduring American fable.

## you gotta know

- Starring *Judy Garland* as Dorothy, with *Ray Bolger* (the Scarecrow), *Bert Lahr* (the Cowardly Lion), and *Jack Haley* (the Tin Man).
- Based on *L. Frank Baum's* 1900 novel *The Wonderful Wizard of Oz*.
- Features the iconic song "Over the Rainbow," performed by Garland, which won the Academy Award for Best Original Song.
- Dorothy's journey with her companions to meet the Wizard teaches lessons about self-discovery: the Scarecrow finds brains, the Lion finds courage, and the Tin Man finds heart.
- Won two Academy Awards: Best Original Song and a special award to Garland for outstanding juvenile performance.
- A massive box-office and critical success that has become a cultural touchstone, broadcast annually on television for generations.
